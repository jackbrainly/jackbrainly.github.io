30. The tourists are accommodated in 3 hotels. There are 8 more tourists in the second hotel than in the first hotel and in the third hotel there are 14 more tourists than in the second hotel. If there is a total of 258 tourists, how many tourists are accommodated in each of the hotels? please show solution. Thanks!


Answer:

[tex]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}First\:hotel:&76\:tourists\\Second\:hotel:&84\:tourists\\Third\:hotel:&98\:tourists\end{array}\right][/tex]

Computation:

Representation

[tex]Number\:of\:tourists\\\\\left[\begin{array}{ccc}First\:hotel&\to&x\\Second\:hotel&\to&x+8\\Third\:hotel&\to&x+8+14\end{array}\right][/tex]

Solving for the number of tourists in each hotel.

[tex]First\:hotel:\\x+x+8+x+8+14=258\\3x+30=258\\3x=258-30\\3x=228\\x=228/3=76\\\\Second\:hotel:\\x+8=76+8=84\\\\Third\:hotel:\\x+8+14=76+8+14=98[/tex]
