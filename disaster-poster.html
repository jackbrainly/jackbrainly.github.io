Identify disaster

One of the disaster that can affect schools and the surrounding community is flooding. The flood calamity that occurred was not just a result of natural causes but people also played a role in it. This is due to the fact that flooding has cut off access to several schools. As a result, many teachers struggle to complete their teaching duties and many pupils fall behind in class. Students can work together in groups to solve difficulties, visiting teachers are required in flood-affected schools or systems, and parents are encouraged to help their children study at home while the flood subsides, albeit this is less productive. With this, it is intended that parents and kids can comprehend the value of preserving the environment for everyone's benefit, including going to school.

The flood disaster that occurred was not just the result of natural causes but people also played a role in its occurrence. They are not accustomed to disposing of rubbish in the trash, which is the cause. Because rubbish clogs the river's waterways, human waste that is not disposed of properly becomes one of the causes of flooding. There are still very few issues brought on by adults' habits of tossing rubbish in the bin and the absence of trash cans in the neighborhood.

Implementing a waste bank will help raise awareness of the importance of throwing rubbish in the trash. The purpose of a waste bank application is to introduce different waste types and their advantages in addition to removing trash from around the river. This is so that the neighborhood would realize not all rubbish is a catastrophe, but there are also advantages. This basic act is anticipated to serve as the foundation for the growth of public awareness.
