30. rita bought a bag of milk and fruit candies weighing 268 grams. The milk candies weigh 4 grams each and the fruit candies weigh 5 grams each. If there are 32 milk candies, how many fruit candies are there in the bag?


First, you multiply the number of milk candies to its weight.
32 X 4 = 128.
Next, you have to subtract the result from the total weight of both candies.
268 - 128 = 140
Lastly, divide the result (140) into the weight of the fruit candies.
140/5 = 28
The answer is there are 28 fruit candies in the bag.x = number of fruit candies
5x = total weight of fruit candies
(4) (32) or 128 = total weight of milk candies
268 = total number of fruit and milk candies combined.

Equation:
Weight of milk candies + weight of fruit candies = 268

128 + 5x = 268
5x = 268 - 128
5x = 140
5x/5 = 140/5
x = 28

ANSWER:  There are 28 fruit candies.
