13. 3 paragraph about your opinion in full face to face classes​


Answer:

Full-face to face classes have the advantage of providing a traditional and personal learning experience for students. In a face-to-face class, students have the opportunity to interact with their teacher and their peers in real-time. This can create a sense of community and shared learning experience, which can be beneficial for students, particularly those who learn best through verbal and visual cues. Additionally, students can ask questions and get immediate feedback, which can help them understand the material better.

However, there are also potential disadvantages to full-face to face classes. They can be disrupted by external factors such as inclement weather or students being absent due to illness, they can be limited by the physical capacity of the classroom. Furthermore, many students may find that full-face to face classes are too demanding, especially in terms of time and travel.

In conclusion, it's important to consider the advantages and disadvantages of full-face to face classes, as well as take into account the needs and preferences of the students, when deciding on the appropriate approach for education. While a full-face to face class is not the only option for learning, it's one that still holds benefits for many students. It's always a good idea to have a blended approach which can incorporate online learning, hybrid classes, or even a self-paced instruction.

Ang pangunahing kahalagahan ng pagtuturo sa buong face to face class ay ang kakayahan ng mga mag-aaral na makipag-ugnayan sa isa't isa at sa guro. Ito ay nagbibigay sa mga mag-aaral ng mas mahusay na pagkakataon upang maunawaan ang mga konsepto sa pamamagitan ng pagpapaliwanag, pagdebate, at pagtatanong. Ang mga klase ng buongong face to face ay binibigyang diin ang pag-aaral sa pamamagitan ng pakikipag-usap at pakikinig sa mga guro at mga kasama sa klase. Ang pag-uusap at pag-uusap ay nagbibigay ng mas mahusay na pag-unawa at mas mahusay na pagtatasa, na nagpapahintulot sa mga mag-aaral na mag-aral ng mas mahusay at mas mahusay na mag-apply ng mga aralin sa buhay.
