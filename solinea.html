1. B. state the converse of the following statements 1. Three non-colinear points determine plare. if it isuoa Three non-colinean points then it is a determine plan


Answer:

The converse of the statement "Three non-colinear points determine a plane" is "If it is a determined plane, then it contains three non-colinear points."

Answer:

Three non-colinear points determine a plane in three-dimensional space. If the points are not collinear, they cannot be on the same line and therefore must be on a plane. This is because in three-dimensional space, a plane is defined as a flat surface that extends infinitely in all directions. It is determined by three non-colinear points because three points are required to define a unique plane in three-dimensional space.

24. are points A and C colinear?why?​


Answer:

Two points are always collinear since we can draw a distinct (one) line through them. Three points are collinear if they lie on the same line. Points A, B, and C are not collinear. We can draw a line through A and B, A and C, and B and C but not a single line through all 3 points.
