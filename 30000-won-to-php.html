3. Mr.Edo invested php 30000 to an account that pays 4 percent annually.how much is the amount of interest at the end of the year?​


PRT:

=====================================

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

r = R/100 = 4%/100 = 0.04 per year,

putting time into years for simplicity,

12 months ÷ 12 months/year = 1 years,

then, solving our equation

I = 30000 × 0.04 × 1 = 1200

I = $ 1,200.00

The simple interest accumulated

on a principal of $ 30,000.00

at a rate of 4% per year

for 1 years (12 months) is $ 1,200.00.
