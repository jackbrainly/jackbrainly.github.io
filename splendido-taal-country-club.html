19. which of the following volcanoes are very familiar in term of eruption?A. Taal, Pinatubo and MayonB. Taal, Pinatubo and BulusanC. Taal, Banahaw and PinatuboD. Taal, Kanlaon and Mayon​


Answer:

Active Volcanoes in the Philippines

Of all the choices presented, the most active volcanoes in the Philippines are Taal, Kanlaon, and Mayon. Therefore, the correct answer is letter D.

Explanation:

Since the Spanish began documenting the history of the Philippines, Taal, Mayon, and Kanlaon volcanoes have erupted more than a dozen times. These three volcanoes are the three most active in the country. The fourth most active volcano in the country is Bulusan, found in Sorsogon. Pinatubo and Banahaw are also active volcanoes being observed and monitored by PHIVOLCS, but they only erupt once in a while. The real threat lies with the volcanoes that are known to erupt frequently.
