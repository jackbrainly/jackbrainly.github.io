Answer:

1. 40 x 80 meters

2. 449 x 153 feet

3. First side = 13 cm

   Second side = 19 cm

   Third side =  24 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Perimeter = 240

Let x be the width; x

Perimeter = 2 (l + w)

length is twice the width; 2x

240 = 2 (2x + x)

We need to eleminate everything in the right side so that only the "x"s are left. First divide both sides by 2:

120 = 2x + x

120 = 3x

40 = x

Length = 2x = 2 (40) = 80 cm

Width = x = 40 cm

Dimension: 40 x 80

2. Perimeter = 1204 feet

Perimeter = 2 (l + w)

Let x be the width; x

Length is 10 feet less than three times the width; 3x - 10

1204 = 2 ((3x - 10) + x)

Again, divide both sides by 2

602 = 3x - 10 + x

Add 10 to both sides so that the 10 on the right side will be canceled out

612 = 3x + x

612 = 4x

153 = x

Width = x = 153 feet

Length = 3x - 10 = 3(153) - 10 = 449 feet

Dimension: 449 x 154 feet

3.  Perimeter = 56 cm

Let x be the second side; x

First side is 6m shorter than the second; x - 6

Third side is 2 cm shorter than twice the length of the first side; 2( x - 6 ) - 2

Perimeter of Triangles = Side1 + side2 + side 3

56 = (x - 6) + x + 2(x - 6) -2

Simplify everything in the right side

56 = x - 6 + x + 2x - 12 - 2

56 = 4x - 20

Add 20 to both sides so 20 will cancel out on the right side and only the "x"s will be left.

76 = 4x

Divide both sides by 4

19 = x

First side = x - 6 = 19 - 6 = 13 cm

Second side = x = 19 cm

Third side = 2(x - 6) - 2 = 2 (19 - 6) - 2 = 24 cm

26. A park is in the shape of an obtuse triangle. One angle is 45⁰, and the opposite side is 280 m long. The longest side is 360 m. What is the area of the park? I need a solution:)


Answer:

17,556.8197 square meters.

Use Law of Sines to find the other two angles:

If theta is the angle opposite of 360, then theta = arcsin((360rt2)/(2x280)) = 114.6136 degrees. (Note: due to Ambiguity of Sines, you will actually get 65.3864 degrees but, since it’s an obtuse angle, you know you want the supplement, 114.6136)

2. The angle we actually want (to calculate area using Area by Sine) is the one between the two given side lengths. 180 - (45 + 114.6136) = 20.3864 degrees.

3. Area by Sine: 280 x 360 x 1/2 x sin(20.3864) = 17,556.8197 square meters
