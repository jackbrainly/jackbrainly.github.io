1. common factors of 54 and 72


Answer:

The common factor of 54 and 72 is 1,2,3,6,9, and 18

Explanation:

The GCF of

72

and

54

is

18

.

Explanation:

One way of finding the GCF of two numbers goes as follows:

Divide the larger number by the smaller to give a quotient and remainder.

If the remainder is

0

then the smaller number is the GCF.

Otherwise repeat with the smaller number and the remainder.

In our example, we find:

72

54

=

1

with remainder

18

54

18

=

3

with remainder

0

So the GCF is

18

.

2 x 2 x 2 x 3 x 3 x 3

2 x 2 x 2 x 3 x 3 x 3
