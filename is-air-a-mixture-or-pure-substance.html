Explanation:

Salt = Pure because ordinary table salt is called sodium chloride. It is considered a pure substance because it has a uniform and definite composition.

Soil = Mix because it is composed of small pieces of a variety of materials, so it is a heterogeneous mixture.

Hydrogen = Pure because it has a definite and constant composition.

Nitrogen = Pure because Nitrogen, is an element. It is composed of NITROGEN. So by definition, it is pure.

Sugar = Pure because table sugar, or sucrose, is not a mixture. It is a compound. Elements are pure substances because they cannot be broken down any more.

Oxygen Point = Pure because oxygen contains only one substance, but air contains several substances.
