18. In an apartment complex, there are 5 windows in each apartment, and 9 apartments in each floor. How many windows are there on 8 floors?


Answer:

Algebraic Equations

In an apartment complex with 5 windows in each apartment and 9 apartments in each floor, there should be 360 windows in 8 floors.

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this equation, we need to list down the items given, and what we are looking for. This way, we can solve the problem easily and in a systematic manner.

Given:

5 windows in each apartment 9 apartments in each floor

Find:

Number of windows on 8 floors

Solution:

Total number of windows = 5 X 9 X 8

Total number of windows = 360 windows

Therefore, on the first 8 floors of the apartment complex, there should be a total of 360 windows.

25. A trapezoidal piece of land has a parallel sides that measures 50 m and 38 m. These sides are 24 m apart. If the land will be divided equally in to 3 lots. What will be the area of each lot?


Answer:

The area of each lot would be 352 square meters.

Computation:

Solving for the area of the trapezoidal piece of land.

[tex]A=\frac{a+b}{2}h=\frac{38\:m+50\:m}{2}(24\:m)=44\:m(24\:m)=1,056\:m^{2}[/tex]

Dividing the area of the land by 3.

[tex]1,056\:m^{2}/3=352\:m^{2}[/tex]
