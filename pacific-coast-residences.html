29. why do ocean currents in the pacific have more effect on the climate of the east coast of the Philippines than the west coast​


Answer:

Up welling brings cold, nutrient-rich water from the depths up to the surface. The earth's rotation and strong seasonal winds push surface water away from some western coasts, so water rises on the western edges of continents to replace it, which is why the ocean is so much colder on the east coast than the west coast.
