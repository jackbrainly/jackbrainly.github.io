23. What is the meaning of lack of contentment


What is Lack of Contentment?

Lack of Contentment is being not fulfilled or unsatisfied with the things that you have possessed. Being not contented is being jealous of what others have owned. Instead of admiring others, you become competitive with them. Soon, you start to hate yourself and strive to possess more than you currently have.

Negative Effects of Lack of Contentment:

Humiliation Permanent anxiety Miserable life Envy Unsatisfaction Hatred Greediness Corruption Theft Discouragement Lack of trust Despair Manifest Pride Rebellion Low self-esteem

Reasons why you are Lacking Contentment :

You have hate and resentment in your heart. You do not value your relationship with God. You don't have enough inspiration. You don't know your priorities in life. You are looking for happiness in the wrong places.  

Ways to Overcome Lack of Contentment :

Change your mindset and attitude. Get some manner of positive behavior. Begin with something that provides you with purpose. Enjoy the things you only have. Choosing to get control is a determination to value ourselves. Spend time with people you love. Start thinking that you can make things better. Work harder to attain your goals. Find good in everything.  Enjoy every moment of your life. Help the lives of others to become better.
