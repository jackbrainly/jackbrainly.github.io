11. What life realizations or applications you can think of while reading the "dot... dot... dot..." reference from daily devotional?


Answer:

ELLIPSIS (dot.. dot... dot...)Daily Devotional, November 5

I realized that even though ellipsis, is usually used when someone is stuttering or is used to express uncertainty when it comes to sentences or dialogues, it has a different meaning when used as a literary device about life. I learned that just like the ellipsis, when we feel like we're giving up or we feel like we want to end things, we should just stop for a while and continue when we can. We shouldn't give up and put a period so easily on things and as much as we can, we should hold on and continue where we left things when we're ready to do so. We should put ellipsis instead of periods so that we would always have a place for endless possibilities and that there's a chance for continuation for the things we stopped halfway.

23. 1. Songs written by the folk and are sung to accompany daily activities such as farming, fishing and putting the baby to sleep. a Devotional Song b. Folk Song d. Kundiman c. Harana


FOLK SONGS

FOLK SONGS were composed by the people during the folk times. Folk songs were usually sung to narrate the daily activities of the folks. Folk songs are important as they make us look back into the past. Folk songs help us understand how life was like hundred years ago and what life our ancestors were living.

Some of the themes of Philippine Folk Songs are:

Farming Fishing Rural life Familial sentiments Courtship Baby lullabies

Some for the famous Philippine Folk Songs are:

Pamulinawen Atin Cu Pung Singsing Bahay Kubo Sarung Banggi Magtanim ay Di Biro Pakitong Kitong Manang Biday Paruparong Bukid Leron Leron Sinta
