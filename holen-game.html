6. ACTIVITY NO. T Directions: What skills are involved or developed in the following games? 1. Patintero 2.luksong tinik 3.holen ​


Answer: ✏

Patintero

Playing patintero will help you develop gross motor skills, hand-eye coordination, and strategic thinking. Skills that are involve are: safe tagging, agility, evasion, balance, spatial awareness.

Luksong Tinik

The skills that are involve in playing luksong tinik are jumping skills, balance and leg strength. The element of health-related and skill-related fitness required for this game is power.

Holen

When playing this game, you either have the skills in rolling the marble into the hole or hitting/shooting an opponents marble to get to the finish line. The skills needed are precision, timing, strategic thinking, etc.
