27. 2. Explain w Ihy it is more difficult to know the pressure at which metamorphic rocks form than the temperature at which they form.​


Explanation:

Because temperature differences between the surrounding rock and the intruded magma are larger at shallow levels in the crust, contact metamorphism is usually referred to as high temperature, low pressure metamorphism. The rock produced is often a fine-grained rock that shows no foliation, called a hornfels.
