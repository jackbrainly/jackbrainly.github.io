1. Summary in the story about bambi​


Explanation:

Bambi is a classic Disney animated film released in 1942, based on the book "Bambi, A Life in the Woods" by Austrian author Felix Salten. The story is set in a forest and follows the life of a young deer named Bambi, from his birth to his first winter, his courtship and marriage, and his eventual role as Great Prince of the Forest.

The story begins with the birth of Bambi, a fawn, and his mother's efforts to protect him from danger. Bambi learns to walk and explore the forest, and makes friends with other animals such as Thumper, a rabbit and Flower, a skunk. Throughout his journey, Bambi has to overcome many challenges, such as hunting season, forest fire and learning to take care of himself.

Bambi is a heartwarming tale about growing up, the beauty of nature, and the importance of family and friends. The film is also known for its powerful message of conservation and the need to protect the natural world.
