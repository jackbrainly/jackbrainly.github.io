3. What is the difference between Kairos & Kronos


What is the difference between Kairos & Kronos

It is one of two words that the ancient Greeks had for time the other being chronos Whereas the latter refers to chronological or sequential time, kairos signifies a proper or opportune time for action. In this sense, while chronos is quantitative, kairos has a qualitative, permanent nature.
