12. Mr. Galvez secured a loan of Php 8,000 from a cooperative bank which charged him 15% interest for 36months . What is the amount paid to the bank?​


Answer:

Php 4,913

Step-by-step explanation:

exponential function: depreciation

A=P(1-r)^t

P=Php 8,000

r=15% = 0.15

t= 36months= 3 yrs | 36÷12=3

substitute:

A=8,000(1-0.15)^3

A=8,000(0.85)^3

A=Php 4,913

you can check it using scientific calculator by typing it directly: "8,000(0.85)^3"
