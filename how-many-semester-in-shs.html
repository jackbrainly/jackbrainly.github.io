13. During one semester, Rica Mae was given 25pesos for each month test that she passed and was fined 50pesos for each math test that she failed. By the end of the semester, Rica passed seven times as many tests as dhe failed and she had a total of 375pesos. How many tedts did she fail?


Rica failed 3 times in the test. To solve this problem, we use a two-variable linear equation.

Step-by-step explanation:

Be discovered:

Rica Mae was given 25pesos for each month test that she passed.

And was fined 50pesos for each math test that she failed.

By the end of the semester, Rica passed seven times as many tests as dhe failed and she had a total of 375pesos.

Question:

How many tets did she fail?

Asked:

Suppose

Pass = X and Fail = Y

X = 7Y

25X - 50Y = 375

25(7Y) - 50Y = 375

175Y - 50Y = 375

125Y = 375

125Y = 375

Y = 375 : 125

Y = 3

Rica failed 3 times in the test.

The linear equation of two variables is an algebraic equation where each variable has the degree of one.

Solving linear equations of two variables can be done by several methods, including the method of elimination, substitution and a combination of the two methods.

Elimination method is a method used to solve or find the solution set of a system of linear equations of two variables by eliminating (eliminating) one of the variables.

The substitution method is a way of solving equations by inserting one equation into another equation.

Step-by-step explanation:

She passed 7 times so

25 x 7 = 175

and she also failed 7 times so

She got 375 pesos so

500 - 375 = 150

so 50 x 3 = 150

30. during the semester break erina read 2/9 of a book on thursday 1/3 of it on friday and the rest 240 pages on saturday how many pages were there in the book


Total number of pages: x

Part of the book read on Thursday: (2/9)x

Part of the book read on Friday: (1/3)x

Remaining pages read on Saturday: 240 Pages

Equation:

(2/9)x + (1/3)x + 240 = x

x - [2/9 + 3/9]x = 240

x - (5/9)x = 240

(9/9)x - (5/9) x = 240

(4/9)x = 240

x = 240 (9/4)

x = 540 ⇒ Total number of pages

ANSWER: 540 pages.

Check:

(2/9)(540) + 1/3(540) + 240 = 540

120 + 180 + 240 = 540

540 = 540 (True)
