21. 1. The population of Bay Village is 35,000 today. Every year the population of Bay Villageincreases by 750 people.a. Write a linear function that represents the population of Bay Village x years fromtoday.​


Answer:

f(x) = 35,000 + 750x

Step-by-step explanation:

Current population: 35,000

Increase in population per year in succeeding x years: 750

Number of years from today: x

Population as function of x years:

f(x) = 35,000 + 750x

To use the function, let's say you want to know the population in 4 years; x= 4:

f(3) = 35,000 + 750(4)

f(3) = 35,000 + 3,000

f(3) = 38,000

27. On a map, the distance between village A and village B measures 6 centimeters and the distance between village A and village C measures 9 centimeters. The actual distance from village A to village B is 20 kilometers. If the distance between two points on a map is directly proportional to the actual distance between the locations, how far is it from village A and village C in kilometers?


Using the idea of ratio and proportion you'll have the distance from A to C as 
[tex] \frac{AC}{9cm} = \frac{20km}{6cm} [/tex]
[tex]AC = \frac{(20km)(9cm)}{6cm} [/tex]
cm will cancel out
AC = 30km
