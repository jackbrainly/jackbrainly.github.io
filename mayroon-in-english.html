27. Translate this into English 1. Minamahal habang mayroon kung wala ay patapun-tapon.2. Nasa Diyos ang awa nasa tao ang gawa.3. Ang gawa sa pagkabata Dala hanggang sa pagtanda.4. Ano man ang gagawin, makapitong iisipin.5. Magsisi ka man sa huli wala nang mangyayari.


Translate this into English.

1.Minamahal habang mayroon

kung wala ay patapun-tapon.

           You love it while it’s around and ignore it when it’s gone.

EXPLANATION

It is like a thing that you own. When you see it around, you use it over and over but when you lost it, you just have to use another thing.  

2. Nasa Diyos ang awa

nasa tao ang gawa.

            Mercy belongs to God while work is up to human.

EXPLANATION

People who work are blessed by God while those who do not work for themselves do not receive the blessings and mercy of God.

3. Ang gawa sa pagkabata

Dala hanggang sa pagtanda.

What you do while you’re young is what you are when you get old.

EXPLANATION

We must raise a child with good character and attitude because what he learns when he is young is the character he will possess in the future when he becomes old.

4. Ano man ang gagawin,

makapitong iisipin.

         Whatever you do, consider the consequences by seven times.

EXPLANATION

Seven times or more is what is meant by this quote. It says that before you decide or do something, you must consider first its consequences whether this action will result to good or bad. Decide on an action that will result best for you and for others.

5. Magsisi ka man sa huli

wala nang mangyayari

      Regrets in the end won’t change what has already happened.

EXPLANATION

It is somehow related to the previous quote. One must think over first of the consequences of every action because if you fail to choose what’s good and better, there is no turning back. There are cases that even how much you regret your action, there is nothing you can do to make things good or better.
