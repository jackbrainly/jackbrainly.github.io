23. Ang bangon ay nangangahulugang?


bangon •

n.rising from a lying positionv.bumangon (-um-) to rise from a lying position, to get up from bed. Bumangon siya nang maaga kanina. He got up early a while ago. magbangon, ibangon (mag-:i-)v.to raise something, to see something in an upright position. Ibangon mo ang may sakit. Help the sick person get up.
