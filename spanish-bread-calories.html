7. A recipe for 20 pieces of spanish bread calls for 12 tablespoons of sugar. How many tablespoons of sugar are needed for 30 pieces of spanish bread?​


PROPORTIONhappens when two ratios are equal

PROBLEM

A recipe for 20 pieces of spanish bread calls for 12 tablespoons of sugar. How many tablespoons of sugar are needed for 30 pieces of spanish bread?

ANSWERYou need 18 tbs of sugar for 30 pieces of spanish bread.

SOLUTION

As 20 peices of spanish bread is equal to 12 tbs of sugar, we can write, 20 : 12,

If there are 30 pieces of spanish bread, how many tbs of sugar is needed? Hence, 30 : x, following the ratio of peices of spanish bread is to number of tbs of sugar.

Thus,

20 : 12 = 30 : x

Solving for x,

[tex] \frac{20}{12} = \frac{30}{x} \\ \\ (\frac{20}{12} = \frac{30}{x})12x \\ \\ 20x = 30 (12) \\ \\ [20x = 30 (12)][ \frac{1}{20} ] \\ \\ x = \frac{30(12)}{20} \\ \\ x = 3(6) \\ \\\boxed{x = 18}[/tex]

Therefore, you need 18 tbs of sugar for 30 pieces of spanish bread.
