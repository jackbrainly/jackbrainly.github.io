27. 1. 35 is what percent of 452. 10 is what percent of 303. 38 is what percent of 120​


✏️PERCENTAGE

===============================

- We make the assumption that 45 is 100% since it is our output value. Then, represent the value we seek with x, when x is to find the percent in this two numbers.

1. 35 is what percent of 45?

[tex] \begin{gathered} \begin{cases}\sf 100\% = 45 \\ \sf x\% = 35\end{cases}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \sf \frac{100\%}{x\%} = \frac{45}{35} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \sf \frac{x\%}{100\%} = \frac{35}{45} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \sf x = 77.78\%[/tex]

- Therefore, 35 is 77.78% of 45

[tex] \large \sf[/tex]

2. 10 is what percent of 30

[tex] \begin{gathered} \begin{cases}\sf 100\% = 30 \\ \sf x\% = 10\end{cases}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \sf \frac{100\%}{x\%} = \frac{30}{10} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \sf \frac{x\%}{100\%} = \frac{10}{30} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \sf x = 33.33\%[/tex]

- Therefore, 10 is 33.33% of 30

[tex] \large \sf[/tex]

3. 38 is what percent of 120

[tex] \begin{gathered} \begin{cases}\sf 100\% = 120 \\ \sf x\% = 38\end{cases}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \sf \frac{100\%}{x\%} = \frac{120}{38} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \sf \frac{x\%}{100\%} = \frac{38}{120} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \sf x = 31.67\%[/tex]

- Therefore, 38 is 31.67% of 120

30. If 30% of a number is 120, what percent of the same number will be 150? WITH SOLUTIONS = BRAINLIEST​


Answer:

37.5%

Step-by-step explanation:

First , we will solve for the number

30% of N = 1200.30 × N = 120N = 120 / 0.30N = 400

Thus , the number is 400

Then , we will solve for the percentage

R = P / BR = 150 / 400R = 0.375R = 0.375 × 100%R = 37.5%

Therefore , the answer is 37.5%
