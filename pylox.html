INGESTION- the process of taking food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it.DIGESTION-the process of breaking down food by mechanical and enzymatic action in the alimentary canal into substances that can be used by the bodyABSORPTION-the process or action by which one thing absorbs or is absorbed by another.
