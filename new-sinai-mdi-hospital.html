What is the significance of the Sinai covenant​?

The Torah informs Jews about the Mount Sinai covenant that God established with all Jews. The Book of Exodus narrates the tale of God deliverance of the Hebrews from Egyptian slavery, with Moses as their human leader. In Judaism, Moses is a crucial prophet. He was selected by God not only to lead the oppressed Jewish people to liberation but also to teach all Jews God rules.

The Mosaic covenant (called after Moses) is a biblical agreement between God and the biblical Israelites, including their proselytes. It is also known as the Sinaitic covenant (named after the biblical Mount Sinai). The mosaic covenant isn't just about the 10 commandments or the time they were given.
