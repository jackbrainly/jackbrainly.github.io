5. What is a comic?help me thx​


Answer:

comic is a medium that expresses narrative or other ideas using a series of still images usually combined with text

Comic strip, series of adjacent drawn images, usually arranged horizontally, that are designed to be read as a narrative or a chronological sequence. The story is usually original in this form. Words may be introduced within or near each image, or they may be dispensed with altogether. If words functionally dominate the image, it then becomes merely illustration to a text.

1.) think of the mood you want your readers to feel. first, you have to figure out the theme and the category, whether it be fantasy, horror, etc.

2.) possess an art style you can claim as your own. don't imitate other artists' style of drawing, rather, create a unique and memorable style that may become the hallmark of your comic.

3.) create a plot or plan the occurences for the comic. remember that comics are still stories, but more visual and may be a handful.

4.) learn the basics, such as framing, speech balloons, characters, narrations, etc..

5.) make sure the comic will be understandable and catch people's interest. create something original and also, make sure that the cover is something that can get stuck in people's minds.
