3.Cold weather fronts usually move from northwest to southeast. The air behind a cold front is colder and drier than the air in front. When a cold front passes through, temperatures can drop more than 15 degrees within an hour. ... When a warm front passes, the air becomes noticeably warmer and more humid than it was before.
