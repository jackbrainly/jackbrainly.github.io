The plants of my mother are not in good condition

Question (Your question based on what you saw)

Why are the plants not in good condition? Could it be because the soil used is not right?

Hypothesis (Your assumed answer to your question)

The soil being used lacks nutrients, thus it needs to be replaced with another pot of soil, which is rich with nutrients and is healthy for the plant.

Experimentation (Testing your hypothesis)

We divide the experimentation into two groups to check whether our hypothesis is true.

Control group: The plant with the old soil of my mother.

Experimental group: The new soil that is fertilized and rich with nutrients.

Analysis (The result of your experimentation)

After a few days, the plant with the fertilized soil has grown to a healthier and better condition than the old soil.

Conclusion (What you can say about your analysis)

The growth of the plant is partly affected by the soil it is planted upon, thus a good soil condition must be maintained in order to keep the plants healthy.
