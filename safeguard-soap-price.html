Answer:

15%

Step-by-step explanation:

The markup rate formula:

Selling price - cost

---------------------------

           cost

103.5 - 90

-------------- = 0.15

    90

Therefore the markup rate is 15%.

The markup is ₱13.5.

The selling price is ₱103.5 (90+13.5)
