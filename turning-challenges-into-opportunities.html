6. turning challenges to opportunities essay examples ​


Turning challenges into opportunities is a real challenge. It is also one of the most important skills for entrepreneurs.

One of the ways I've found to turn challenges into opportunities is by using my business as an example. I'm always looking for ways to make the most of my business, and one way is to look at what my customers are telling me about their problems and how I can help them overcome those problems.

14. turning challenges to opportunity your journey:​


Answer:Challenges are an inevitable part of life. Whether it be challenging times, people or circumstances – most of us can relate to coming up against roadblocks in life that feel hard, unfair, or even devastating at times.

Some people face the most unthinkable tragedies and somehow come out the other side stronger. Others crumble under the weight of the pressure and heartache they face. The same challenge and circumstance one person might rise up from, could become someone else’s lifelong misery they never recover from.

In times of struggle, having a resilient spirit becomes an asset that seems to transform challenges into opportunities for growth.

The question then becomes: how do we learn and grow from our challenges instead of becoming trapped or swallowed by them? How do we shape that transformation to help us move forward, instead of leaving us stuck?

While there is no obvious or “one size fits all” solution to becoming more resilient, these are some of the ways that have helped me turn my challenges into opportunities for growth and self-transformation:

Perspective

The lens we view our world through is as individual as we are. What we see and believe to be true, is not “the” truth, it is “our” truth. If the truth we live by no longer serves us, or has us coming up against the same hard lesson over and over – perhaps it’s time to look at adopting a different perspective that “will” move us forward.

Changing our minds about something can be an extremely liberating act that expands our horizons. Looking at the same issue through a different lens has a way of opening up new doors of possibility. This shift can actually help us see things from a fresh and hopeful perspective.

When we practice looking at life from different viewpoints, it becomes more natural to feel compassion for other people and for ourselves. With perspective, we can come closer to finding the lesson or opportunity for growth in just about any situation.

16. Turning challenges into opportunities essay


When writing an essay, you have to make an outline about the topic. Plan how you will write your introduction, what you will be pointing out in the body, and how you will end your essay using the conclusion. Make sure that every main point has supporting details as well. Here's a sample essay on turning challenges into opportunities:

"Facing challenges is no easy feat for many. It may set us back from our goals or it may even make us feel unmotivated. Although there are little challenges and big challenges, it is how you look at them that you'll be able to overcome them. It is when we see challenges as opportunities that we will be able to properly face them and overcome them.

People face challenges in their everyday lives--from small inconveniences to large setbacks. But instead of looking at them solely as enemies, we can try to shift our perspective and ask ourselves how these challenges can benefit us. Just like one fresh graduate who kept getting turned down at numerous job interviews. When he looked at these challenges as an opportunity for him to learn what he's lacking and how he can improve, he overcame the obstacle and finally got a job.

Challenges may make us feel bad about ourselves, but always remember that it's okay to feel bad temporarily and use challenges as opportunities to improve yourself."

18. persuasive essay about turning challenges to opportunities


Persuasive essay about turning your challenges into opportunities:



To write a persuasive essay, first, you must know the definition of persuasive. Persuasive means being “convincing”. Thus it means you must convince your readers, convince them that they can turn their challenges into opportunities.



Here’s a very short example of persuasive essay about turning challenges to opportunities:



“I was challenged by a professor before in a marketing class. He said: How can you sell your product - which is ice cream, by the way - in Baguio… during freezing point in December?” Then I froze. That was a challenge, right? How can you sell a freezing product during a freezing season?! I had to answer that question before I got a failing mark for the recitation. My scholarship was at risk.



After a minute or two of thinking… and freezing, I had finally answered it and got a perfect score. My scholarship was saved. Actually, I learned from my own answer. I was also surprised by it. And even these days, I still apply what I’ve learned from my own adrenaline rush and use that technique in my marketing strategies. That’s my “turning challenges into opportunities example”. Challenges must not break us, they must make us. We learn from obstacles and it makes us smarter, braver, and better. It must be an opportunity to grow and be wiser.  


So don’t be afraid of these challenges. Face them and accept your fate. The metamorphosis! You will be a much better version of yourself when you face and solve these challenges. It’s scary. Yes, it is. But if you will always allow yourself to have a pair of frozen feet, what will be your progress? Let the metamorphosis turn you into a butterfly. Still curious about my answer to my prof? I said… “Well, I’ll build a warm coziest place in the city with such magnificent fireplace for them to enjoy the ambiance and taste my yummy ice creams.” Now, I am going to open an ice cream house at Baguio.”
