30. anu ang ibig sabihin ng kundol?​


Answer:

Explanation:

Kundol is one of the vegetables mentioned in the Filipino folk song Bahay Kubo, which every Filipino learns to know by heart from childhood. It’s a great mnemonic for learning about vegetables, particularly those native to the Philippines.

Kundol is also a popular filling for the Chinese-Filipino treat called hopia. Wintermelon cubes are slow-cooked in pork fat or lard to be used as filling in hopiang baboy (“pork” hopia)
