1. what is the mongol golden horde empire? ​


Answer:

The Mongol Golden Horde Empire was a powerful empire that existed from the 13th to the 14th centuries and ruled over much of Central Asia and Eastern Europe. It was founded by Genghis Khan and his descendants and was one of the largest empires in world history. The Golden Horde's territories included modern-day Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, and parts of Central Asia and the Middle East. The Golden Horde was known for its military might and its successful campaigns of conquest, which allowed them to expand their empire and gain control of vast amounts of wealth and resources.
