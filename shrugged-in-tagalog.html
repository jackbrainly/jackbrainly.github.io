19. Based on the language of Kinesics, what does ,,shrug" mean?​


Answer:

Shrug means "I don't know" in kinesics

Explanation:

The Shrug is a gesture performed by raising your two shoulders. The classic shrug, with one-off raising and lowering of shoulders usually means 'I don't know!' and may be accompanied with raised eyebrows, down-turned mouth, and hands held to the side, with palms upwards or forwards (showing nothing is being concealed).
