5. tina and grace are friends.the sum of their ages is 27.if tina is 2 years older than grace how old is grace​


Answer:

12 yrs and a half.

Step-by-step explanation:

tina = X

grace = Y

equation 1

X + Y = 27

equation 2

X = Y + 2

substitute eq2 to eq1

Y + 2 + Y = 27

2Y = 27 - 2

2Y = 25

divide both sides with 2

Y = 12.5 or 12 years and a half.

23. nothing is worse than nothing explain the statement above. ​.


"Worse than nothing" means that it would be better to have nothing at all than accept the option available.

An acknowledgment that a particular thing, although not ideal, is preferable to nothing at all.

If you say that something is better than nothing, you mean that it is not what is required, but that it is better to have that thing than to have nothing at all. After all, 15 minutes of exercise is better than nothing. See full dictionary entry for nothing.
