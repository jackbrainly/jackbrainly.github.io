A Spaniard thought we were very like the Spanish; an American thought we were Dot American enough. A Frenchman remarked we were the only people in Asia with a sense of humor, at least the only ones who could laugh at themselves, which, when one thinks of it, was probably the prettiest compliment of all. By way of contrasts, our fellow Asians had a uniform tendency to laugh at us. A Siamese said we were pretentious.
