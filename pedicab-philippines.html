Equation:" V2=V1 +at "... Where V2= final velocity; V1= initial velocity (0); a= acceleration ( 2m/s^2); t=time (6 seconds)....substitute and solve: V2= 0 + (6*2) = 12 m/s--- answer. Next, find distance : d= (1/2 )at^2... d= (1/2)*2*6^2.... d= (1/2)*2*36= (1/2)* 72 = 36 meters..---answer

3. what you must do when riding a pedicab & don't do when riding a pedicab?​


Answer:

When riding a pedicab, there are several things you should do to ensure your safety and the safety of others on the road. There are also certain things you should avoid doing to prevent accidents and other incidents. Here are some guidelines to keep in mind:

DO:

Wear a helmet: Always wear a helmet when riding a pedicab to protect your head in case of an accident.

Sit comfortably: Sit comfortably on the pedicab and hold on to the handrails to maintain your balance.

Keep your feet inside: Keep your feet inside the pedicab and avoid dangling them outside, as they could get caught in the wheels.

Communicate with the driver: If you need to make a stop or have any concerns, communicate with the pedicab driver to ensure a safe and smooth ride.

Follow traffic rules: Follow traffic rules and signals, just as you would when driving or walking.

Be aware of your surroundings. Keep an eye out for pedestrians, other vehicles, and obstacles in your path.

DON'T:

Overload the pedicab: Do not overload the pedicab with too many people or too much weight, as this can affect its stability and safety.

Lean outside the pedicab: Do not lean outside the pedicab, as this can cause it to tip over.

Use electronic devices: Do not use electronic devices while riding a pedicab, as this can be distracting and unsafe.

Take unnecessary risks: Avoid taking unnecessary risks or attempting stunts on a pedicab, as this can lead to accidents and injuries.

Engage in unruly behavior: Do not engage in unruly or disruptive behavior while riding a pedicab, as this can put yourself and others in danger.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a safe and enjoyable ride on a pedicab. Remember to always prioritize your safety and the safety of others on the road.
