27. What is the distance between an airplane that is 890 m above the sea level and a submarine that is 102 m below sea level?​


ANSWER :The distance between an airplane that is 890 m above sea level and a submarine that is 102 m below sea level is 992 m. This is because the sea level is the reference point for measuring the altitude of both the objects. The airplane is 890 m above the sea level, and the submarine is 102 m below the sea level. Therefore, the difference between the two altitudes is 890 m - (-102 m) = 992 m.
