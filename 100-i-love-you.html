Actual deeprooted love takes time, and when it finally happens, you start feeling their pain and happiness just like your own. You start feeling that the other person is just an extension of you. Soon as this person is out of your sight there is this longing deep down in your core too see them again, to feel their warmth near you.

Now coming back to the definition of love, we can never define love, because it's a feeling and no amount of words can sustain the enormity of emotions love can make you feel.
