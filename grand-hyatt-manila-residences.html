24. pangasinan is 191 kilometers from manila how many meters are you going to travel if you want to visit your grand mother in pangasinan


Answer:

The answer is 191,000 meters.

Explanation:

Meters of Travel From Manila to Pangasinan

In the given, it was stated that Pangasinan is 191 kilometers (km) away from Manila. It is important to note that for every kilometer, there are 1,000 meters (1 km is equal to 1,000 m).  If somebody is traveling from Manila to Pangasinan to visit his or her grandmother, it will take him or her approximately 191,000 meters (m) to get to the province. To be able to understand more, a solution and how the answer is generated will be explained below

How to Convert Kilometers to Meters?Determine the given:Distance from Manila to Pangasinan = 191 kmIn the metric system, it is established that for every 1 km, there are 1,000 m.Perform the solution:To get the answer, write the formula: 191 km x 1,000 m / 1 kmTo be able to get the right unit, write the unit to be cancelled under the possible unit. This is to cancel the same units which are inversely proportional to each other in the equation.Calculate the answers based on the formula: 191 km times 1,000 m divided by 1 km. Cancel the km units to be left with m. The answer is 191,000.To explain further, if 1 km is equal to 1,000 m, then 191 km must be equal to 191 multiples of 1,000 m.

29. sino ang mga arkitektong amerikano na gumawa ng grand plan of manila?​


Answer:

Daniel Hudson Burnham, FAIA (Setyembre 4, 1846 - Hunyo 1, 1912) ay isang Amerikanong arkitekto at taga-disenyo ng lunsod. Isang tagapagtaguyod ng kilusang Beaux-Arts, maaaring siya ay, "ang pinakamatagumpay na power broker na ginawa ng propesyon ng arkitektura ng Amerika."

Noong 1905, iniharap ni Burnham ang kanyang plano para sa Maynila, na naka-pattern sa Paris, Venice, at Naples.

Isang matagumpay na arkitekto ng Chicago, siya ay napili bilang Direktor ng Mga Trabaho para sa 1892–93 World's Columbian Exposition, na colloquially na tinutukoy bilang "The White City". Nagkaroon siya ng mga prominenteng tungkulin sa paglikha ng mga master plan para sa pagpapaunlad ng ilang lungsod, kabilang ang Plan of Chicago, at mga plano para sa Manila, Baguio at downtown Washington, DC Nagdisenyo din siya ng ilang sikat na gusali, kabilang ang ilang kilalang skyscraper sa Chicago, ang Flatiron Building na may tatsulok na hugis sa New York City, Union Station sa Washington DC, Selfridges department store ng London, at Merchants Exchange ng San Francisco.

Bagama't kilala sa kanyang mga skyscraper, pagpaplano ng lungsod, at para sa White City, halos isang-katlo ng kabuuang output ng Burnham – 14.7 milyong square feet (1.37 million square meters) – ay binubuo ng mga gusali para sa pamimili.
