15. Think of a person of another religion, sex, or social class. How can you make him/her feel welcome?​


Answer:

The steps to make a person of another religion, sex, or social class feel welcome include showing respect and empathy, being friendly and approachable, learning about their culture, sharing your own experiences, and being inclusive. Treating them with kindness, respect, and empathy, and creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and accepted are the keys to making someone feel welcome.
